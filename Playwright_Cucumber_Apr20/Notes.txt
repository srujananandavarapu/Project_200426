BDD
====
BDD: Behavioural driven developement

in code:
========
expect(title).toBe('Login Page')

English
========
Then I should see page title as "Login Page"

Why we need to implement BDD?
==============================
To bridge the gap between:
Business people (Product Owners, Managers)
Developers
Testers

Everyone can understand the same test cases because they are written in plain language.

Advantages of BDD
===================
✅ Easy to understand for non-technical people.
✅ Improves collaboration between Business + Dev + QA.
✅ Requirements are clear and in testable format.
✅ Test cases are reusable and act as documentation.
✅ Reduces miscommunication.

Disadvantages of BDD
=====================
❌ Needs extra setup (Cucumber, feature files, step definitions).
❌ Writing scenarios in plain English takes more time.
❌ If not maintained properly → Scenarios become too many and confusing.
❌ Sometimes technical teams feel it’s too much overhead 
if business team is not really involved.

configure  Cucumber
====================
npm i @cucumber/cucumber --save-dev
or
npm install @cucumber/cucumber -D

Hooks
=====
BeforeAll => 1 time
Before => n times depends on no of scenarios
BeforeStep => n times depends on no of statements on each scenario
AfterStep => n times depends on no of statements on each scenario
After => n times depends on no of scenarios
AfterAll => 1 time
